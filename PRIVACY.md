# Privacy

## What this app stores

This app stores:

- the local contact list used by the launcher
- a hashed admin PIN
- local settings such as kiosk mode and contact-editing preferences

All of that data is stored on-device.

## What this app does not do

This app does not currently include:

- a remote backend
- cloud sync
- analytics
- advertising
- account login

## External apps used

The launcher hands off calling and SMS to the system dialer and system messaging app already installed on the phone. Those apps may have their own privacy behavior and policies.

## Scope of this note

This is a simple open-source prototype privacy note, not legal advice and not a store-ready policy document.
